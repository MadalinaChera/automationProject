2025: verifying relogging into Gmail 

Preconditions: Already have an active Gmail address.
		In the past, you must have been connected and saved your Gmail username and password.

Steps:

1. Write on your Google Chrome browser "gmail.com" and press the Enter key.
 Expected results: The Login page appears
		Your account is already there. (e.g. mail.madalina.chera@gmail.com)

2. Select your account with a left click.
Expected results: The password field appears with a blank space.

3. Write the password in the "Enter your password" field.
Expected results: The field is now completed with your password marked with *. (e.g. *******)

4. Click the "Next" button.
Expected results: The Gmail page is opened.
